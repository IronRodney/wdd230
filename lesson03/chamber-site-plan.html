Kgatleng Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Purpose

This site provides a comprehensive business directory that showcases local businesses in our district.

This space is to also facilitate networking among chamber members by providing a platform for connecting businesses, organizing networking events, and promoting collaboration.

Scenarios

We represent the interests of local businesses to government authorities.

We are the voice of the business community on issues affecting commerce in the region.

We provide workshops, seminars, and trading programs to enhance the skills and knowledge of local entrepreneurs.

We promote local businesses through marketing campaigns. both locally and potentially on a broader scale.

We also work towards the economic development of Kgatleng through strategic initiatives and partnerships.
